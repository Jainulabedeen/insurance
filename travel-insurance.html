ABNIC Travel Insurance UAE

A Comprehensive solution, Wherever you travel, it travels with you.

ABNIC Travel Insurance UAE is a comprehensive insurance plan that caters to travelers worldwide. The policy provides wide range of benefits including but not limited to Personal Accident, Medical Expenses, delayed flights, hotel cancellation, baggage delay, medical evacuation, Trip Cancellation or Travel Delay and more. ABNIC Travel Insurance UAE unparalleled service quality and proven operational capabilities allow our customers to receive the best-in-class care. ABNIC caters both for outbound and inbound travelers with exclusive coverage at competitive costs.

Whether a business trip, a family vacation or a leisurely holiday our Outbound Travel plan is best suited for all kinds of travelers. has a suitable plan for every kind of traveller. ABNIC offers Two plans Gold and Silver catering to both Individual and Family with a wide range of benefits to protect your travel.

Key Features:

ABNIC Travel Insurance is designed to protect you during abroad trips

  •   Coverage for both Individual & Family includes (Husband + Spouse + 8 Children) with COVID-19 benefits.
  •   Age limit cover up to 80 years.
  •   Children up to 18 years are charged 50% of the adult premium; and above 18 years charged adult premium.
  •   Options to choose from Winter Sports & Terrorism Cover with additional loading on basic premium.
  •   Annual Cover (Multi-trip): Any single trip limited to maximum period shall not exceed 92 consecutive days.
  •   Single premium for family cover (Husband + Spouse + 8 Children).
  •   Online policy issuance through portal facility.
  •   Travel warnings / limitations issued and monitored by the Foreign & Commonwealth Office should be adhered to at all times (https://www.gov.uk/foreign-travel-advice).Claims are not covered if travel undertaken against the advice of FCO.
  •   Claims related to war, terrorism, invasion, act of foreign enemy, hostilities (whether war is declared or not) civil war, civil commotion, rebellion, revolution, insurrection, military force, coup d’etat, weapons of mass destruction are not covered.
  •   Read the policy wordings for all other terms and conditions.

Covers

ABNIC Travel Insurance policies are designed to o suit your travel requirements, be it for a short holiday a long vacation or business trip, ABNIC Travel Insurance has 3 plans that best suits your needs.

  •   Worldwide Including USA & Canada
  •   Worldwide Excluding USA & Canada
  •   Schengen+UK
  •   Schengen Only

Our Inbound travel plan is designed for visitors travelling on short or long term visits to UAE during their stay the policy coverage provides you emergency medical expenses due to accident or emergency case within UAE.

Covers:

  •   Room and Board: General ward
  •   Healthcare services for emergency cases
  •   Treatments, surgeries, anesthesia and other services
  •   Ambulance fees for emergency cases only
  •   Medicine necessary for the treatment of the emergency case during hospitalization
  •   Repatriation of mortal remains

Our Inbound plan may be ideal for you if you plan on spending of your holiday in UAE, and are under the age of 80 years old at the time the policy is issued. There is no cover for Pre-existing medical conditions and chronic diseases. Insurance takes effect at the date of entry to the country and the policy expires on the expiring date of this insurance or once the visitor leaves country. For any emergency medical assistance have access to our 24 hours a day and 365 days a year to apply for assistance, so we’re ready to help you out when unexpected issues arise during your trip.
